What is Crios?

A crios is a multicolored woven woolen belt. In a similar sense, the Crios project is a story woven with diverse characters and told through multiple mediums—video games, tabletop games, comics, audio dramas, novels, animation, and more.

The goal is not only to entertain but to craft something that uses storytelling in every medium to tell a story that can only be told through that medium. A universe where harmonic resonance governs reality, where realms converge, and where the line between hero and antagonist blurs.

— Davisem, Creator of the Crios Project
