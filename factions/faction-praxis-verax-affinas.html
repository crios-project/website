Overview

Praxis Verax Affinas (PVA) was an experimental inter-realm exploration organization founded within Nexsicon. The group's central objective was to validate the controversial Lodesoul Theorem, which proposed that realities possess hierarchical metaphysical density known as Ontological Depth, and that these realities can be integrated under specific narrative and magical conditions.

Originally conceived as a scientific and exploratory initiative, PVA gradually evolved into an active realm-integration task force. Their operations resulted in Nexsicon absorbing multiple smaller dimensional constructs, significantly increasing its metaphysical stability and scale.

Despite its early successes, the organization ultimately collapsed following a catastrophic causality rupture known as the Cascade Resonance, which fragmented alliances, destabilized reality structures, and triggered internal ideological division.

Foundational Purpose

PVA existed to:

  • Validate the existence of multiple realms
  • Prove the legitimacy of Lodesoul Theory, originally developed by Tiala's disgraced father
  • Study and manipulate Ontological Depth relationships
  • Facilitate controlled integration of weaker or unstable realms into Nexsicon
  • Establish safe cross-realm navigation and magical identity stabilization systems

While initially framed as academic and preservationist, their work gradually shifted toward large-scale metaphysical intervention.

Operational Achievements

PVA accomplished several unprecedented feats:

  • Successful integration of multiple minor realms into Nexsicon
  • Stabilization of cross-realm travel corridors
  • Development of Magical Persona Partitioning, allowing members to operate openly while preserving civilian identity integrity
  • Early refinement of inter-realm magical girl transformation frameworks
  • Ontological Depth amplification of Nexsicon through controlled realm absorption

The Antagonist Methodology

To reveal and stimulate Lodesoul manifestations, Tiala adopted antagonist identities across different realms:

  • dissonant discord — Harmonic destabilization entity operating in Beeston
  • BLADE GUILLOTINE — Catastrophic execution archetype operating in Royastella

These identities were deliberately constructed as existential threats designed to force Lodesoul activation and measurable metaphysical response.

The Cascade Resonance

Following successful identification of Lodesoul presence across multiple realms, Tiala attempted to forcibly accelerate universal entropy convergence through an unassisted Grand Magic designed to merge all realities into the realm with the highest Ontological Depth.

The spell succeeded in partially destabilizing dimensional boundaries but produced unintended consequences:

  • Narrative probability failures across multiple realities
  • Cross-realm entity displacement
  • Spontaneous emergence of historically impossible events
  • Accelerated metaphysical entropy
  • Breakdown of causal continuity

Organizational Collapse

The Cascade Resonance triggered several irreversible events:

  • The unexplained disappearance of Xīwàng Tiānshàng
  • Escalating emotional and ideological conflict among members
  • Increased instability across operational realms
  • Loss of centralized leadership coherence

Tensions escalated beyond reconciliation, culminating in:

  • Ami abandoning PVA
  • Tiala restructuring remaining members into Destiny's Penance
  • Ami forming Reason Theory in ideological opposition

These events marked the effective dissolution of Praxis Verax Affinas.

Successor Factions

The remnants of PVA split into two opposing organizations:

Destiny's Penance
Led by Tiala, continuing the convergence mission with moral accountability. Members: Tiala, Jí Lián, Claris, Velia.
Reason Theory
Co-founded by Ami and Windi, opposing destiny manipulation through sabotage and disruption. Anti-Tiala collective.

Legacy

Despite its collapse, PVA fundamentally reshaped cross-realm metaphysics. Their work:

  • Validated Ontological Depth as a measurable phenomenon
  • Established foundational cross-realm transformation theory
  • Triggered multiple modern magical faction formations
  • Introduced long-term instability into inter-reality causality

Many current interdimensional crises trace their origin directly to PVA experimentation.

Symbolism

Praxis Verax Affinas represents:

  • The danger of knowledge pursued without restraint
  • Scientific ambition colliding with emotional consequence
  • The fragility of friendship under cosmic-scale responsibility
  • The ethical ambiguity of salvation through destruction
