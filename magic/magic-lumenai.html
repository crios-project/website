Overview

Lúmenai is not spellcasting in the traditional sense, but harmonic resonance: magic arises when intent, emotion, and will align into a coherent voice. Magic is sung, not spoken. Power is measured not by mana quantity, but by harmonic stability and resonant authority.

All Lúmenai manifestations are categorized by Essentia, structured into ascending harmonic strata known as Tiers.

Fundamental Principles

  • Lúmenai responds to emotional truth, not deception.
  • Higher Tiers require sustained resonance, not quick casting.
  • Power escalation increases cognitive strain, identity destabilization, and reality feedback.
  • No voice exists in isolation — resonance affects allies, enemies, and the realm itself.

Tier 1 — Basic Elements

These are the foundational harmonic modes. All Lúmenai casters begin here.

Éthera — Free / Nonelemental
raw will, unshaped intent, magical breath
Barriers, force projection, dispels, spell frameworks, harmonic amplification. Éthera defines control, not power.
Pýrua — Fire
ignition, passion, consumption
Flames, explosive release, emotional surges, destructive momentum.
Áquora — Water
memory, flow, adaptation
Healing scaffolds, emotional recall, fluid manipulation, persistence magic.
Vaélia — Wind
motion, pressure, direction
Speed, pressure waves, mobility, aerial control.
Téroma — Earth
weight, form, endurance
Defense, structural magic, grounding, fortification.

Supremacies — High Harmonic Domains

Supremacies override elemental logic. They cannot be fast-cast and require long-form resonance.

Tier 3 — Lúmira (Light / Holy / Healing)

Essence: restorative truth. Healing is re-harmonization, not repair.

Common Effects: Healing, purification, revelation, oath-binding. Excessive use risks emotional collapse due to enforced truth.

Tier 4 — Ómura (Dark / Gravity / Collapse)

Essence: inevitability, absence, pull. Darkness is not evil — it is weight and silence.

Common Effects: Gravity wells, void shaping, suppression fields, memory erasure. Extended exposure destabilizes identity and reality cohesion.

Tier 5 — Æónel (Neon Ultra / Infinity)

Essence: impossible continuation. Æónel cannot be sustained by a single voice.

Common Effects: Reality recursion, infinite constructs, temporal loops, conceptual alteration. Most casters burn out, fragment, ascend, or erase themselves.

Narrative Notes

  • Lúmenai is not morally aligned. Power reveals truth — it does not create it.
  • The Lovely Hearts function as harmonic ensembles, amplifying each other.
  • dissonant discord weaponizes disharmony, forcing Supremacy collapse.
  • Tier access is story-gated, not skill-gated.
  • Supremacy use alters long-term character trajectories.
  • Æónel usage permanently changes canon reality states.
