Magic Systems

Arcane Frameworks of the Crios Multireality

Three distinct magic systems operate within the Crios cosmology. Each system obeys different metaphysical rules and cannot be freely substituted for another. When forced to interact, they create reality shear events that permanently alter the fabric of existence.
Beeston

Lúmenai

Harmonic resonance magic powered by emotional truth and ensemble synchronization. Magic is sung, not spoken.

Fuel:Sound Resonance
Access:Voice & Harmony
Risk:Emotional / Existential
Royastella

Element Core

System-regulated mana magic bound to identity-based elemental cores. Reliable, rigid, quantifiable, regulated,and combat-optimized.

Fuel:Mana from System
Access:System Authorization
Risk:Mechanical / Identity
Nexsicon

Cymora

Linguistic reality-shaping through spoken incantations. Words perform magic rather than describe it. Structured by semantic coherence and grammatical syntax.

Fuel:Semantic Coherence
Access:Spoken Cymora
Risk:Conceptual / Reality
