Secrets of the Multiverse

Delve into the foundational concepts, cosmic phenomena, and artifacts that define the CRIOS multiverse.

Akashic Record
The primordial archive containing the total memory, structure, and probability pathways of existence across all realities. A cosmic library of every event made and unmade.
Metaphysical • Archive
Aspects of Infinity
Tangible instantiations of conceptual constants—physical manifestations of abstract concepts like Death, War, Chaos, and Fate that can be integrated and channeled.
Concept • Power Source
Cascade Resonance
The largest recorded multiversal destabilization event triggered by Tiala Hartlight's experimental Grand Magic designed to forcibly accelerate universal convergence.
Event • Crisis
Light Blade Revolver
Multi-configuration combat weapon engineered for Alulu Dayk. Dynamically shifts between ranged suppression, surgical melee strikes, and high-output execution modes.
Weapon • Technology
Lodesoul Theorem
The cosmological theory proposing the existence of singular individuals whose presence stabilizes, defines, or influences the trajectory of their native universe.
Theory • Cosmology
Paradox Bracers
Paired fusion relics capable of merging two compatible individuals into a singular, newly emergent entity designated as a Novus. Complete ontological integration.
Artifact • Fusion
Realm Convergence
The large-scale cosmological phenomenon where independent realities begin gravitationally, narratively, and metaphysically merging into a single shared existence.
Phenomenon • Convergence
Tiānshàng Legacy
A hereditary honor system where the clan surname must be earned through existential merit. Ascension manifests through the Six-Star Installation in the eyes.
Lineage • Transformation
