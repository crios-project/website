Overview

Marate Jalo stands as the mentor, failsafe, and final safeguard of Starlite 7th. Known as the Gold Imperator, he embodies accumulated mastery, moral authority, and overwhelming combat supremacy.

Unlike other Runic Knights, Marate exists in a permanent state of Mana Surge, granting him constant operational readiness and unparalleled power stability.

Despite his immense strength, Marate almost never engages in combat. His role is preservation, guidance, and intervention only during catastrophic scenarios where the defeat of Starlite 7th is imminent.

Personality

Core Traits

  • Deeply Compassionate: Measures people by integrity, not strength
  • Fiercely Protective: Especially of the orphans he raises
  • Charismatically Commanding: Effortless natural leadership
  • Philosophically Disciplined: Strong ethical framework
  • Honorable to a Fault: Deliberately handicaps himself for fairness

Marate is widely beloved within his community for his warmth, accessibility, and magnetic personality. His charisma is effortless rather than cultivated, drawing trust from strangers and loyalty from allies.

He personally oversees and raises a collective household of orphans, treating each child as family and investing heavily in their emotional and educational development.

Personal Philosophy

Marate believes conflict should be honest, balanced, and worthy of both participants. He routinely handicaps himself during combat to preserve fairness. Most of his scars are the result of deliberately limiting his own advantages.

To Marate, victory gained through overwhelming imbalance is hollow. He maintains extensive awareness of global power movements and factional maneuvering—not from paranoia, but from a deeply rooted belief that stability must be quietly maintained.

Combat Profile

Starlit Alpha functions as the apex authority among Runic Knights. His presence symbolizes the System's ultimate contingency against existential collapse.

Royal Greaves

A pair of enhanced shin guards that serve as both weapon and mobility amplifier.

  • Massive kinetic force amplification
  • Extreme acceleration boosts
  • Vertical and horizontal mobility expansion
  • Devastating close-range kick-based combat

Marate integrates Royal Greaves into a combat style centered around devastating lower-body striking techniques and aerial engagement.

Starlight Blessing: Universal Blessing Access

Marate possesses unrestricted access to every Starlight Blessing utilized by other Runic Knights.

  • Tactical adaptability across all engagement types
  • Ability to mirror or counter allied or enemy Blessing usage
  • Seamless switching between combat disciplines

Role

Marate acts as:

  • Instructor
  • Moral anchor
  • Emergency battlefield equalizer
  • Keeper of institutional memory

He combines mastery across every weapon category with devastating hand-to-hand combat, often favoring brass knuckles, barehanded combat, and weapon improvisation.

Heaven's Mandate

Activation Condition: Unlocks when all other Runic Knights have fallen or been rendered combat ineffective. Activates when Starlit Alpha successfully teleports all the fallem Runic Knights back to the Starlight Citidel and collects their weapons

Effect: Royal Greaves transform into a full-body combat system incorporating all Starlit Weapons.

Operational Enhancements

  • Multi-weapon integration
  • Maximum mobility augmentation
  • System-level combat synchronization
  • Absolute battlefield dominance

Heaven's Mandate is considered the final defensive protocol of the Runic System. This shift engenders the transition from Starlit Alpha into The Gold Imperator.

The Final Safeguard

The Gold Imperator functions as the embodiment of last-resort justice and generational continuity. When all else has fallen, when Starlite 7th can no longer protect what must be protected, Marate Jalo becomes the final line between collapse and salvation.

In this state, restraint gives way to absolute necessity. The man who handicaps himself for fairness becomes the unstoppable force that ensures survival.
