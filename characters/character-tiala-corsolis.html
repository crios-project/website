Overview

Tiala Corsolis exists less as a conventional individual and more as a transitional constant moving between realms. She rarely manifests directly within a single plane of existence, instead expressing herself through alternate presences that emerge across different worlds. These manifestations operate as localized avatars through which her influence is exerted, often without the surrounding populations recognizing their shared origin.

Known Manifestations:

  • dissonant discord within Beeston
  • BLADE GUILLOTINE within Royastella
  • Alulu Caelum within Nexsicon

While these incarnations possess independent personalities, memories, and motivations, they remain intrinsically linked through an underlying metaphysical continuity anchored in Tiala's existence.

Appearance

Tiala presents as a teenage girl of slender but balanced build. Her physical presence carries an understated intensity, often described as quietly radiant. Her purple eyes are particularly striking, frequently perceived as holding layered emotional and perceptual depth that can make sustained eye contact unsettling or mesmerizing.

Her demeanor tends toward calm observation rather than overt emotional expression, contributing to the sense that she is perpetually slightly out of sync with her immediate surroundings.

Nature and Existence

Tiala functions as a liminal entity, naturally positioned between states, identities, and dimensional boundaries. Rather than remaining fixed within a single reality, she traverses existential thresholds through phasewalking. These transitions are rarely witnessed directly and frequently leave behind residual distortions in magical, emotional, or conceptual space.

Cross-Manifestation Consistencies

Her alternate manifestations do not simply represent disguises or transformations. Each operates as a fully realized iteration shaped by the laws, magic systems, and narrative pressures of its host realm. Despite this fragmentation, certain thematic consistencies persist:

  • Strong associations with culmination or finality
  • Exceptional combat lethality
  • Emotional intensity masked beneath controlled composure
  • A recurring role in events involving systemic upheaval or paradigm shifts

Magical Alignment

Tiala's true elemental or metaphysical alignment remains unclassified. Each known manifestation interacts with its respective world's magical structure differently:

  • In Beeston: Discordant harmonic anomalies tied to Lúmenai resonance disruption
  • In Royastella: Extreme Dark-aligned phenomena and catastrophic combat expression
  • In Nexsicon: Highly precise and destructive applications of Cymoran combat methodology

No singular system fully explains or contains her existence, suggesting her origin lies beyond standard elemental or systemic categorization.

Personality

Direct observations of Tiala herself are exceedingly rare, making definitive personality profiling difficult. However, consistent behavioral threads across her manifestations suggest:

  • Deep Introspection: Constantly analyzing existence and identity
  • Restrained Emotion: Strong internal currents held beneath calm composure
  • Gravitational Pull: Naturally drawn toward pivotal or climactic events
  • Identity Complexity: Complex relationship with continuity and self-fragmentation

Combat Profile

Alulu Caelum employs a precision-based executioner combat doctrine centered on calculated lethality, structural targeting, and overwhelming single-strike termination. Her methodology prioritizes efficiency over spectacle, focusing on identifying critical vulnerabilities and exploiting them with decisive, devastating blows.

Executionist

A specialized slashing épée engineered for both thrusting penetration and razor-edged cutting arcs. Unlike traditional épée combat, which emphasizes point control and linear fencing exchanges, Alulu integrates extended slashing trajectories that allow her to sever tendons, disable joints, and compromise armor seams with surgical accuracy.

  • Rapid transitions between piercing attacks and sweeping execution strokes
  • Hybrid dueling and assassination capabilities
  • Designed for fatal accuracy and minimal wasted motion

Note: She is actively seeking a better weapon as part of her grand plan.

Combat Methodology

Tactical Approach

Alulu demonstrates exceptional patience and battlefield composure. Rather than engaging in prolonged exchanges, she studies opponent movement patterns, guard transitions, and stance imbalances. She deliberately pressures enemies into predictable defensive habits before delivering catastrophic counterstrikes.

Target Priority

Her attacks frequently target anatomical or structural weak points:

  • Ligaments and tendons
  • Nerve clusters
  • Pressure channels
  • Magical focal nodes

Movement Philosophy

Her footwork is disciplined and economical, drawing from formal duelist traditions. She maintains strict centerline dominance, advancing with controlled, deliberate steps designed to funnel opponents into restricted movement corridors.

When pressured, she relies on precise parries and micro-adjustments rather than wide evasive maneuvers, conserving energy while maintaining lethal readiness.

Offensive Bursts

Alulu's offensive bursts are sudden and explosive. She is known to remain nearly motionless for extended periods before unleashing rapid multi-vector strikes that collapse an opponent's defenses in moments. These bursts often culminate in finishing blows delivered with extreme velocity and force concentration.

Psychological Warfare

Alulu exerts intimidation through composure and inevitability. She rarely telegraphs emotion during battle, projecting an aura of calm finality that unsettles opponents. Her fighting presence communicates a silent promise: every engagement is a measured countdown to execution.

Limitations

While devastating in direct duels, Alulu's style demands acute situational awareness and timing. Extended chaotic combat environments or unpredictable multi-target engagements can disrupt her precision rhythm, requiring her to adapt by creating space or isolating high-priority threats before delivering conclusive strikes.
