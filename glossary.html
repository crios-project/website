GLOSSARY

A comprehensive guide to the terminology, concepts, and phenomena of the CRIOS multireality

ABCDE FGHIL MNOPR STUVZ

A

Akashic Record
Cosmic Archive
The primordial archive encompassing the collective memory, structural blueprint, and probability pathways of existence across all dimensions of reality. Functioning as a non-linear informational lattice, the Record preserves every event, thought, and entity as resonance data, including the echoes of potential futures. Due to its foundational nature, accessing the Record is widely regarded as a feat of impossible complexity, carrying the risk of catastrophic cosmological collapse. It serves as the ultimate convergence objective for Tiala Hartlight, representing the pinnacle of her pursuit to interface with the fundamental source of all information.
Ambrasis
Artifact
An evolving blade adorned with dark roses, serving as the primary conduit for Mahana Sahi (Heart Symphony) to channel her escalating power. The weapon acts as a catalyst for her spiritual and physical evolution, facilitating her ascension to the Heart Crescendo state and eventually her ultimate form, Finalis Grande, through the essential assistance of Celesin. Reflecting the growth of its wielder, Ambrasis’s physical form shifts over time, becoming increasingly ornate and complex as the user’s power reaches new heights of resonance.
Antiquity, Realms of
Realm
A vast collection of background dimensions within the multiverse characterized by low ontological density. These realms are inherently liminal, existing within a restricted conceptual or physical scope rather than as fully realized universes. While some sectors of Antiquity remain stable enough for reliable access and navigation, others are dangerously volatile, frequently prone to sudden structural collapse or radical transformation. They function as the shifting, peripheral foundations of reality, often overlooked due to their transitional nature.
Ascension Artifact
Transformation Catalyst
A category of rare and exceptionally potent items designed to catalyze the transformational evolution of magical girls. These artifacts act as metaphysical conduits, enabling their wielders to transcend standard power limits and ascend into higher ontological forms. Distinctively, an Ascension Artifact possesses a reactive physical state, growing more visually elaborate and ornate in direct proportion to the wielder’s increasing resonance. Notable examples include the twinned swords Coralas and Ambrasis, which serve as the primary instruments for such evolutionary breakthroughs.
Aspects of Infinity
Metaphysical Concept
Tangible instantiations of conceptual constants that prove every abstraction possesses a physical manifestation somewhere within existence. These entities represent the material reality of concepts such as Truth, Chaos, or War, though they frequently flicker in and out of accessible dimensions. Interaction with an Aspect is categorized into three distinct levels of integration: Augeo (joining), Exsequi (yielding), and Vitio (sublimation). These manifestations are uniquely harnessed through Nexsicon magical transformations, a specialized discipline that operates on principles entirely separate from all other known magic systems.
Augeo
Aspect Integration Level
The foundational state of Aspect integration, characterized by the establishment of a stable connection between an individual and a conceptual constant. In this stage, the user remains fundamentally themselves, with their personality and identity largely intact, though functionally amplified by the Aspect's influence. Augeo grants access to minor manifestation abilities with no intrinsic metaphysical backlash upon disengagement. It represents a state of "joining" where the user acts as a conscious vessel for the Aspect’s power without sacrificing their autonomy.
Aura Manifestation
Elemental Core Advancement Technique
An advanced manifestation of elemental mastery where the user’s internal energy becomes both visible and tangible. Upon activation, the individual’s essence radiates from their form as a physical glow or shimmering aura, making their metaphysical presence perceptible to those nearby. This state is traditionally associated with a heightened level of magical sensitivity, granting the user the refined perception necessary to detect and interact with otherworldly phenomena that remain invisible to standard senses.

B

Beeston
Realm
A realm governed by Lúmenai magic, a domain where metaphysical power is manifested through emotional harmonic resonance. In this reality, spells are sung rather than spoken, as magic users channel Essentia through the convergence of vocal frequency and emotional sincerity. Beeston is the original home of the Lovely Hearts faction and is defined by a distinct "musical magical girl" aesthetic. Transformations within this realm are not merely physical shifts but are outward reflections of a caster's internal harmonic state, with the complexity of their form signaling the depth of their emotional alignment.
BLADE GUILLOTINE
Transformation Identity
The alternate identity of dissonant discord, a persona unveiled during the catastrophic Falling Stars Incident. Characterized by her haunting speech in rhyming couplets and the wielding of the blade Le Couteau, she served as the primary architect behind the rift that empowered the Crystallines. This breach ultimately led to the total structural collapse of the Royastellan System. Utilizing a sophisticated vocal modulator to mask her true origins, her identity remained a secret until her strategic betrayal of Starlite 7th, an event that fundamentally altered the power balance of the multiverse.

C

Calamity
Collective Term
The formal collective noun used to designate a group of Crystallines. This term reflects the inherent danger and destabilizing influence these entities exert when gathered in significant numbers, often serving as a precursor to localized ontological structural failure or widespread destruction.
Cascade Resonance
Catastrophic Event
A reality-altering cataclysm that fractured dimensional boundaries and triggered widespread convergence phenomena across the multiverse. The event is most notably marked by the massacre of the Tiānshàng clan and the onset of severe identity fractures across numerous individuals. Beyond its immediate casualties, the Resonance accelerated the merging of disparate realms, leading to chronic multiversal instability. While the precise cause and total scope of the event remain subjects of intense investigation, its lingering effects continue to destabilize the ontological foundations of existing systems.
Celestial Charge
Magical Enhancement
A specialized power enhancement utilized by the Starlite 7th to transcend standard combat limitations. This state acts as a foundational amplifier, granting the user access to a suite of advanced techniques and significantly heightened skill sets. In the heat of engagement, a Celestial Charge serves as the critical threshold for executing high-level maneuvers that would otherwise be inaccessible.
Convergence
Metaphysical Phenomenon
The metaphysical process by which separate realities merge and overlap, typically triggered by the relocation of a Lodesoul or massive resonance disturbances. This phenomenon causes ancient mythic realms to rematerialize, dimensional boundaries to weaken, and distinct magical systems—such as Lúmenai, Cymora, and the Element Core System—to interact and conflict. The phenomenon is central to the strategy of Tiala Hartlight, who seeks to orchestrate a deliberate, controlled merger of realms to breach the protective layers surrounding the Akashic Record.
Coralas
Artifact
A legendary winged sword and Ascension Artifact that serves as the primary conduit for Heart Aria to evolve her magical state. By facilitating transformational resonance, the blade allows its wielder to ascend to the form of Heart Beat and ultimately reach the Concerto Pulse threshold. This progression is made possible through the critical assistance of Celesin, who aids in stabilizing the massive energy output required for such a transition. Reflecting the growth of its user, Coralas undergoes a physical metamorphosis, becoming increasingly ornate and elaborate as the wielder’s power and harmonic synchronization intensify.
Crystallines
Enemy Faction
A faction of hostile entities foreign to Royastella whose threat level escalated dramatically following the Regicide incident. When BLADE GUILLOTINE opened a catastrophic rift in the sky, these entities underwent a sudden power surge, overwhelming the realm’s natural defenses. This rapid escalation was a calculated maneuver to force the Starlite 7th into seeking combat training from dissonant discord. By accepting her tutelage to combat the growing calamities of Crystallines, the Runic Knights unknowingly succumbed to a long-term manipulation that eventually led to the systematic collapse of their entire world.
Cymora
Magic System
A verbal-only magical language native to the realm of Nexsicon where the act of speaking performs magic rather than simply describing it. Operating on principles of sonic resonance, the language utilizes phonetics influenced by Welsh, Gaelic, and Arabic to create a system where there is no distinction between description and execution. Spells typically follow rigid four-line incantational structures that emphasize rhythm, cadence, and specific vowel elongation to manifest effects. Because the language itself is the conduit, Cymora does not utilize the tiers or elemental cores found in other realms; instead, it relies entirely on a practitioner's capacity to master the foundational resonance required for each specific incantation.
Cymoran Practitioners
Magic User
Unlike the rigid hierarchical structures of other magic systems, Cymoran practitioners do not adhere to a tier-based classification of power. Their ability is governed strictly by rigorous training and the individual’s internal capacity to withstand the specific sonic resonance of a spell. In this system, any practitioner can theoretically cast any incantation, provided they have mastered the necessary rhythm, cadence, and vowel elongation required to bridge the gap between description and execution. This makes the discipline one of the most egalitarian yet demanding forms of magic, as the only true barrier to entry is the caster's own vocal and mental endurance.

D

Demons
Enemy Faction
Hostile entities assualting the realm of Beeston that primarily seek to pillage and plunder resources to transport back to their home dimension. Unlike the Crystallines of Royastella, these invaders are categorized into a rigid hierarchy of increasing power, progressing from Demon Elite and Demon General to the far more formidable Demon King and Demon Emperor tiers. Collectively referred to as a Hatred, these forces are perpetually opposed by the Heart Melody faction, whose harmonic magic serves as the primary defense against their incursions..
Destiny's Penance
Faction
A Nexsicon faction composed of individuals who wield transformations directly aligned with the Aspects of Infinity. Under the leadership of Tiala, the group operates with the singular, radical goal of merging all existing realms into a unified whole. This strategy is not driven by conquest, but by a perceived necessity to fortify the multireality against an impending, "oncoming storm." By consolidating the disparate magical systems and ontological structures of the various realms, Destiny’s Penance seeks to create a singular, defensible front capable of surviving in the ultimate convergence.
dissonant discord
Transformation Identity
A formidable rival and eventual antagonist to Heart Melody, later revealed to be an alternate identity of the catastrophic BLADE GUILLOTINE. Operating as a corrupted harmonic opposite to the Lovely Hearts, she notably speaks in structured iambic pentameter and utilizes the blade Atonality. Her combat style is characterized by wide, flowing slashing arcs that effectively blur the line between high-level fencing technique and ritual casting. The lowercase styling of her name serves as a linguistic reflection of her nature as a fundamental disruption to the established harmonic order of the multiverse..
Divine Mandate
Magical Enhancement
An exclusive and high-tier power enhancement utilized by Starlit Alpha. This state represents a total synchronization with the highest principles of the Royastellan System, allowing the user to ascend into the form of the Gold Imperator. Upon activation, the transformation manifests a distinct Neon Ultra aura, signifying a reach into the supreme metaphysical domains. Unlike standard enhancements, the Divine Mandate bypasses typical energy constraints, granting the user the authority to wield reality-distorting properties that are normally reserved for the most advanced ontological entities.

E

Element Core
Magic System
The identity-bound mana system native to Royastella where elemental energies crystallize within an individual's soul architecture. Users store this mana in a metaphysical organ known as a Magic Core, with all output and ability usage governed by the System’s authorization. While native cores such as Fire, Water, Wind, and Earth provide a stable foundation for the realm's defenders, non-native cores introduced by invaders are inherently more volatile and difficult to regulate. This system operated under the Core Immutability Doctrine—the belief that one's elemental nature was fixed at birth—until dissonant discord shattered this reality by demonstrating Resonant Recomposition, proving that core signatures can be rewritten through extreme harmonic exposure.
Essentia
Lúmenai Term
The harmonic elements channeled through the Lúmenai magic system of Beeston. Unlike the elemental cores of Royastella, Essentia are fundamental resonance modes that respond to emotional sincerity and require harmonic stability to manifest safely. The system is organized into tiers, beginning with the Five Open Voices: Éthera (Neutral), Pýrua (Fire), Áquora (Water), Vaélia (Wind), and Téroma (Earth). Higher-tier manifestations, known as Vaelóri entwinements, combine these base forms into more complex and potent structures. Because this magic is tied to the caster's internal state, any artificial emotional projection or lack of sincerity weakens the output and increases the risk of emotional fatigue or existential failure.
Exsequi
Aspect Integration Level
The intermediate state of Aspect integration, characterized by a dramatic surge in power and the partial erosion of the user's baseline humanity. Unlike the foundational Augeo state, an individual in the Exsequi phase does not merely wield their conceptual power; they must actively feed it. This integration level imposes a strict behavioral constraint: the user must act in accordance with the nature of their chosen Aspect during manifestation. Failure to maintain this philosophical alignment results in a severe, potentially debilitating backlash upon releasing the transformation, as the Aspect demands a toll for the reality-altering influence it provides.

F

Falling Stars
Event
The catastrophic turning point in the multiversal timeline where dissonant discord revealed her true nature as BLADE GUILLOTINE. During this event, she systematically dismantled the Starlite 7th and kidnapped Starlit Alpha through a dimensional rift. The battle concluded with the total collapse of Royastella’s System, an event that permanently neutralized the realm's defenders by causing their magical costumes to fade and their weapons to vanish. This structural failure left the entire population defenseless and doomed, marking the successful execution of discord's long-term manipulation.
Five Open Voices
Lúmenai Term
The foundational tier of Lúmenai magic represents the five basic elements of harmonic resonance that serve as the building blocks for all higher-tier musical manifestations. Unlike the identity-bound cores found in Royastella, these primary Essentia are accessible to any practitioner capable of achieving the necessary emotional sincerity and vocal stability. The classification consists of Éthera (Neutral), Pýrua (Fire), Áquora (Water), Vaélia (Wind), and Téroma (Earth). Mastery of these individual voices is an absolute prerequisite for advancing to Harmonist status, where two or more of these foundational elements are entwined to form complex Vaelóri manifestations.

H

Harmonic Ascension
Transformation Evolution
The specialized process by which magical girls from the realm of Beeston evolve their transformations to access higher-tier abilities and increased resonance power. This evolution is typically facilitated by Ascension Artifacts such as Coralas and Ambrasis, which physically manifest the wielder’s internal growth by becoming more elaborate as their power increases. Through this process, Heart Aria can ascend to the form of Heart Beat and eventually reach the Concerto Pulse threshold, while Heart Symphony can progress to Heart Crescendo and ultimately Finalis Grande. This progression represents a total synchronization of emotional sincerity and musical frequency, often requiring external assistance from entities like Celesin to stabilize the transition.
Harmonist
Magic User
The secondary tier of Lúmenail practitioners, representing a significant shift from the raw, unshaped intent of the Melodist level toward disciplined, surgical precision. A Harmonist is capable of simultaneous voicings, an advanced technique that allows two or more casters to synchronize their foundational elements to form a cohesive Vaelóri Essentia. This stage of mastery requires not only individual emotional sincerity but also an acute awareness of external resonance, as the practitioners must blend their distinct harmonic frequencies without causing dissonance. By achieving this state, the casters can manifest complex elemental combinations like Sýlphae (Lightning) or Crýalune (Ice), which are far more potent and stable than the volatile, "leaky" manifestations of lower-level users.
Hatred
Collective Term
The collective term used to describe a group of Demons in the realm of Beeston. Much like a Calamity refers to a gathering of Crystallines in Royastella, a Hatred represents a concentrated force of these hostile entities as they attempt to pillage and plunder dimensional resources. The term reflects the inherent nature of the demonic hierarchy, ranging from the Demon Elite to the Demon Emperor, and signifies the discordant, aggressive resonance they bring into the multireality.
Heart Melody
Faction
The primary faction from Beeston, also known as the Lovely Hearts. Members undergo transformations through emotional-harmonic synchronization and wield weapons strictly aligned with musical terminology. The group functions as a harmonic ensemble where individual voices strengthen their collective resonance, making their power interdependent. They are particularly notable for their confrontation with dissonant discord, a battle that tragically resulted in the death of Heart Aria.

L

Light Blade Revolver
Artifact
Alulu Dayk's modular transforming weapon is aligned with Neon Ultra energy, granting it reality-distorting properties that bypass conventional defensive paradigms. The weapon is capable of cycling through seven distinct configurations instantaneously: handgun, shotgun, kama-gun, light sword, light broadsword, sniper rifle, and scythe. Because of its alignment with Neon Ultra, each strike carries the potential for bypassing defenses entirely, making it one of the most versatile and dangerous artifacts in the multireality.
Lodesoul
Metaphysical Anchor
A metaphysical convergence anchor tied to specific individuals. According to the Lodesoul Theorem, moving a Lodesoul forces associated realities to gravitationally merge toward the host realm. Tiala's convergence strategy involves relocating multiple Lodesouls to Nexsicon to trigger a controlled realm merger, with the ultimate goal of accessing mythic domains that protect Akashic resonance keys.
Lovely Hearts
Faction Collective
The collective term for Beeston's musical magical girl factions, primarily Heart Melody. Members channel Lúmenai through emotional harmonic resonance, with their physical transformations serving as a direct reflection of their internal musical and emotional states. Their combat style is distinct for its emphasis on ensemble coordination and resonant amplification, allowing individual voices to strengthen a collective field. Notably, the Lovely Hearts do not conform to the standard tiers or restrictions of Lúmenail casting; because of this unique interaction with harmonic resonance, they cannot be categorized as normal practitioners within the traditional system.
Lúmenai
Magic System
The harmonic resonance magic system native to Beeston, where power is manifested through song rather than spoken incantations. This system operates on the principles of emotional truth and vocal harmony; any attempt at artificial emotional projection or a lack of sincerity significantly weakens the resulting magical output.

The system is structured into distinct tiers, beginning with basic Essentia and culminating in supreme manifestations like Æónel (Neon Ultra). Practitioners can also perform Vaelóri entwinements, which involve the complex merging of multiple base elements. However, the risk to the user scales with the power tier, ranging from standard emotional fatigue to total existential failure.

M

Melodist
Magic User
The entry-level stage of Lúmenail harmonic resonance, where the caster focuses on unshaped intent and raw elemental modes. Practitioners at this level are characterized by volatile, "leaky" manifestations, as their control over the resonance is not yet refined enough to contain the output efficiently. Consequently, engaging in magic as a Melodist results in a high degree of emotional fatigue, as the practitioner must compensate for this lack of technical precision with raw internal effort.
Moonlight Curse
Ability
BBLADE GUILLOTINE's signature suppression technique, which integrates directly with her weapon, Le Couteau, to significantly enhance her combat effectiveness. When activated, the curse creates specialized zones of darkness that serve a dual purpose: they act as a tangible physical force capable of striking or restraining opponents, while simultaneously causing profound perceptual disruption to those caught within them. It directly opposes Starlight Blessings.

N

Neon Ultra
Supreme Element
The fifth tier supreme element representing reality recursion and the manifestation of infinite constructs. Within the Lúmenai magic system, this element is known as Æónel. Due to its immense power and its nature as a reality-distorting force, it carries an extreme risk of existential failure for the practitioner. The Light Blade Revolver’s alignment with Neon Ultra is what grants the weapon its unique properties, allowing it to bypass conventional defensive paradigms by manipulating the fabric of reality itself.
Nexsicon
Realm
The realm governed by Cymora verbal magic and the primary home to Aspects of Infinity integration. Due to its inherently transitory nature and high ontological depth, Nexsicon has been designated as the convergence nucleus for Tiala’s strategy to access the Akashic Record. Magical girls within this realm channel conceptual forces through three distinct integration levels, resulting in some of the most philosophically complex and reality-bending magic found anywhere in the multireality.

P

Paradox Bracers
Artifact
These unique transformation artifacts enable two magical girls to merge their essences, allowing them to share a single, unified magical girl form. A critical feature of the Paradox Bracers is the thin, luminous tether that physically links them during the transformation. If this connection is severed or broken, the fusion is immediately terminated, resulting in the instantaneous reversion of both girls to their individual original forms.

R

Reason Theory
Faction
This faction is characterized by a deeply philosophical approach to both combat and magic. Notable members include Amaranthine Hartfall, who wields the blade Fate, and Kweliana Winderial la Pax, who wields the blade Secret. Both practitioners utilize weapons with reality-altering properties that are directly tied to these metaphysical abstractions, allowing them to manipulate the battlefield through conceptual dominance rather than simple physical or elemental force.
Resonant Recomposition
Phenomenon
The process by which Element Cores can be fundamentally altered, allowing the core's base element to be changed through exposure to extreme harmonic resonance. This phenomenon effectively shattered the Core Immutability Doctrine, which previously held that a magical identity was fixed at the point of crystallization. dissonant discord was the one to reveal this capability to Starlit 7th, proving that core signatures are rewritable and that magical nature is not a permanent state.
Royastella
Realm
The realm governed by the Element Core System and the primary home of the Starlite 7th. This world features identity-bound mana storage located within metaphysical Elemental Cores, with all magical output strictly governed by System authorization. During the Falling Stars incident, the realm's central System suffered a catastrophic collapse. This event effectively ended all magical transformation capabilities, stripping the defenders of their power and leaving the entire population defenseless against external threats.
Runic Knights
Faction Collective
The formal designation for the Starlite 7th, identifying them as System-authorized magical warriors. Following the specialized training provided by discord, the group achieved a significant breakthrough: they learned to manifest their elemental cores as tangible, physical auras. This development dramatically enhanced their combat capabilities through Aura Manifestation. Their training also granted them the unprecedented ability to alter the fundamental nature of their Elemental Cores.

S

Sanguina
Race
A former human that has been inflicted with vampirism that has overcome the bloodlust and desires that accompany it. With enough training, a sanguina can even overcome their weakness to the sun. Sanguina are physically superior to normal humans in almost every sense. Notable sanguina include Shini Thana, the Starlit Sultana, and Mahana Sahi, Heart Symphony. Mahana was immediately able to overcome her sun weakness, which is how Shini realized it was even possible.
seven silent blades
Weapon Set
A set of seven weapons wielded by Zhòu-chún Tǎoyàn across different combat roles, consisting of ānjìng (knight's broadsword), ciúnas (ninja's interlocking katanas), tăcere (assassin's gunblade), senyap (runic sword for spellcasting), kimya (dancer's fluid jian), cupa (absolute-cutting katana), and shiru (flying sword). Each blade reflects a different combat philosophy while maintaining the silent blade designation through lowercase styling. This diverse arsenal allows for a fluid transition between fighting styles, from heavy-handed knightly strikes and silent assassinations to aerial support and absolute-cutting precision.
Six-Star Installation
Genetic Phenomenon
The ocular manifestation that appears when an individual earns the Tiānshàng surname. This physical change is marked by six distinct star icons appearing in each iris, arranged with three in the upper right and three in the lower left to create a diagonal bilateral symmetry. Beyond its visual prominence, the installation represents a profound synchronization with the Tiānshàng Legacy. It grants the bearer enhanced perceptual awareness, possible transformation compatibility, and direct access to ancestral memories, effectively bridging the gap between the individual and the cumulative power of their lineage.
Starlite 7th
Faction
The premier Runic Knight team of Royastella, consisting of seven members: Empress the leader, Sultana, Rex, Pasha, Czar, Mikado, and Dynast, all mentored by Starlit Alpha. Their struggle began in earnest after BLADE strengthened the Crystallines by opening the rift in the sky, a shift that forced the team into a defensive, losing battle for survival.

They eventually received training from discord, which granted them the ability to manifest their cores externally as well as changing the element entirely; however, this fortune was a calculated deception. They were being unknowingly manipulated toward their own systematic destruction, a process that culminated in the catastrophic collapse of the realm's magic system during the Falling Stars event.
Starbright
Empowering Material
A high-probability drop from a defeated Zodiarcal. System Mana masters use them to increase capacity for System-based abilities. The Starlite 7th uses them to enhance Starlight Blessings, traditionally limited to starbrights matching their specific elemental core. By learning to change their core's element through discord's training, the Runic Knights can utilize a wider variety of starbrights to raise their core levels beyond previously known limits.
Starlight Arsenal
Weapon Set
A collection of seven weapons wielded by the Starlite 7th, each designed for specific combat roles. The arsenal is a symbol of their collective power and unity.
Starlight Blessing
Skill
A high-level skill suite granted to the Starlite 7th by the Royastellan System. Unlike spells in other magic systems, these unique individual abilities are powered by the System's internal energy rather than external mana. This distinction allows Starlight Blessings to function independently of conventional magical constraints, granting the knights specialized combat enhancements and reality-defying techniques that operate outside the standard laws of sorcery.
Starlight Citadel
Location
The primary headquarters and base of operations for the Starlite 7th. It serves as a sanctuary and tactical hub within Royastella. In the event that the Runic Knights fall in battle, Starlit Alpha utilizes a specialized emergency protocol to teleport them back to the Citadel for recovery and regrouping.
Supremacy Magics
Magic
The pinnacle of magical tiers, representing the absolute apex of power within the multireality. This classification consists of three distinct levels: Tier 3 Lúmira (Light magic), Tier 4 Ómura (Dark magic), and the supreme Tier 5 Æónel, also known interchangeably as Neon Ultra or Aether. Each tier represents a fundamental escalation in metaphysical complexity and the ability to command conceptual reality.
Symphalist
Magic User
The absolute apex of Lúmenai casting, where the practitioner wields Resonant Authority to override standard elemental logic. A Symphalist is capable of solo-casting complex magic that typically requires the synchronized effort of multiple users. At their highest level, they move beyond temporary casting into long-form resonance, allowing them to enact permanent alterations to reality itself.
System (The)
Metaphysical Infrastructure
The governing magical framework of Royastella responsible for authorizing transformations, managing mana distribution, and maintaining combat capability. The System suffered a catastrophic collapse following BLADE's rift manipulation and the subsequent kidnapping of Starlit Alpha. This failure left the Runic Knights entirely powerless, causing their magical costumes to fade and their weapons to vanish as the metaphysical architecture supporting them dissolved.
System Adepts
Magic User
The baseline classification for users of the Royastellan System. Unlike the specialized Runic Knights, Adepts are limited to general System abilities, such as basic mana manipulation and standard authorized functions. They lack the high-level access required for unique Starlight Blessings or advanced elemental manifestations.
System Master
Magic User
MAn advanced tier of user capable of wielding the Royastellan System with high-level proficiency. Unlike Adepts, Masters possess the technical and magical depth to manipulate the framework in unexpected ways, bypassing standard protocols or optimizing System functions beyond their intended parameters. This mastery often allows for greater efficiency in mana usage and the execution of complex System-based abilities.

T

Tiānshàng Legacy
Clan Tradition
A hereditary yet non-automatic honor system governing the Tiānshàng lineage. Children born into the family receive provisional Circumstance Names and only earn the formal surname after proving themselves through existential merit. This transition triggers the Six-Star Installation in the eyes, symbolizing a shift from individual identity to lineage continuity. The system operates on the core principles of merit over blood, contribution over survival, and the preservation of the collective legacy over the self.

U

Ultimatera
Realm
The final realm in the convergence cycle where the ultimate existential conflicts of the multiverse manifest. Details regarding its geography and internal laws remain deliberately obscured, as it represents the endgame scenarios and final confrontations in the evolution of multireality. It is the terminal point where all narrative and metaphysical threads are destined to collide.
Untethered
Entity Classification
Beings who exist without standard reality anchors or connections to an origin realm. This status often results in anomalies within local frameworks; for example, Mahana Sahi arrived in Royastella as an Untethered entity with amnesia. Despite the lack of a known history, Untethered individuals often display exceptional stats and extraordinary potential once they are properly integrated into a realm's specific architecture, such as the Royastellan System.

V

Vaelóri
Magic Term
Tier 2 conjoined elements in Lúmenai formed by entwining two base Essentia. This stage requires mastery of component elements and increased harmonic control to achieve successful fusion. Recognized combinations include Nóxira (Poison from Fire and Water), Férroma (Metal from Fire and Earth), Sýlphae (Lightning from Fire and Wind), Crýalune (Ice from Water and Wind), Víratha (Plant from Water and Earth), and Kórelis (Martial from Earth and Wind).
Vitio
Aspect Integration Level
The terminal state of Aspect integration characterized by the complete subsumption of the original personality. In this state, the individual becomes a total incarnation of the Aspect, experiencing no backlash upon disengagement because the boundary between self and power has vanished. Repeated access to Vitio fundamentally rewrites cognition; the resulting corruption is logical rather than emotional, causing absolute adherence to Aspect principles to appear increasingly rational to the user. Reverting from this state is considered incredibly difficult.

Z

Character
The wielder of the seven silent blades, capable of manifesting different combat personas through distinct weaponry. Each blade represents a specific fighting philosophy and role designation: ānjìng (knight), ciúnas (ninja), tăcere (assassin), senyap (runic swordmaster), kimya (blade dancer), cupa (ruthless striker), and shiru (aerial support). By cycling through these blades, she adapts her entire tactical approach and persona to suit the demands of the engagement.

Enemy Creature
High-tier boss Crystalline based on celestial archetypes. Upon destruction, they have a high probability of dropping starbrights, which are essential for the Runic Knights to increase their ability capacity. These creatures represent significant power bottlenecks and serve as primary catalysts for the growth of Starlite 7th.
