ASPECTS OF INFINITY

"There is nothing that does not exist somewhere as itself."

Ontological Principle

All things possess manifestation.

Not merely matter.
Not merely energy.
Not merely spirit.

Even abstraction has body.

There is:

  • A physical Truth
  • A physical Chaos
  • A physical War
  • A physical Hate
  • A physical Death

Even concepts that already possess metaphysical presence also possess manifested presence.

There is:

  • A physical Red
  • A physical Blue
  • A physical Silence
  • A physical Fate

These manifestations are known collectively as Aspects of Infinity.

They are tangible instantiations of conceptual constants — ephemeral yet real. They flicker in and out of accessible reality, rarely encountered, almost never understood.

But they can be found.

And they can be joined.

Manifestation and Channeling

If one is:

  • Diligent in research
  • Reckless in ambition
  • Or intimately connected to those who are

It becomes possible not merely to find an Aspect —
but to integrate with it.

This is what makes Nexsican magical transformations unique.

The magical girls of Nexsicon do not merely cast spells.

They channel Infinity.

That is why they are powerful.

That is why they are dangerous.

Levels of Integration

Aspect integration progresses through three escalating states:

I. Augeo — The Joining

The foundational state.

  • Establishes connection between individual and Aspect
  • Grants access to minor manifestation abilities
  • Personality remains largely intact
  • No intrinsic backlash for disengagement

The user remains themselves — but amplified.

II. Exsequi — The Yielding

The intermediate state.

  • Dramatically increased Aspect output
  • Partial erosion of baseline humanity
  • The user must act in accordance with the Aspect

Failure to satisfy the Aspect's nature during channeling results in backlash upon release.

Example: An Exsequi aligned with Fire must burn something during manifestation. If they do not, the Aspect will reclaim equilibrium violently.

An Exsequi does not merely wield power. They must feed it.

III. Vitio — Sublimation

The terminal state.

  • Complete subsumption of original personality
  • The individual becomes an incarnation of the Aspect
  • No backlash upon disengagement
  • Actions are fully guided by Aspect logic

There is no resistance at Vitio. Because there is no separation.

The danger lies not in release — but in permanence.

Repeated Vitio access rewrites cognition. A Vitio of Fire will increasingly view combustion as the most rational solution to conflict. A Vitio of War will interpret peace as stagnation. A Vitio of Entropy will see collapse as mercy.

The corruption is not emotional. It is logical.

Strategic Implications

Aspect integration creates exponential combat potential.

However:

  • Vitio overuse produces permanent cognitive distortion
  • Multiple Aspects interacting creates resonance instability
  • Aspect conflicts can trigger metaphysical feedback events

In a realm approaching convergence,
Aspect bearers are not merely combatants.

They are fault lines.

Philosophical Consideration

If every concept has a body —

Then somewhere:

There is a physical Infinity.

And if that is true —

It may already be watching.
