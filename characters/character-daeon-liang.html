Overview

Daeon Liàng is a prodigious financial magnate and tactical savant whose intellect manifests through relentless precision, pragmatic realism, and uncompromising professionalism.

Through disciplined analysis and predictive modeling, he has accumulated immense wealth at a remarkably young age, positioning himself among the most financially powerful individuals in his nation. Unknown to the public, he operates as the primary strategic architect of Starlite 7th.

Personality

Core Traits

  • Hyper-Analytical: Processes situations through rigorous logic
  • Ruthlessly Pragmatic: Prioritizes results over sentiment
  • Emotionally Restrained: Severe control over emotional expression
  • Efficiency-Driven: Demands optimization in all endeavors

Behavioral Traits

  • Does not smile
  • Does not raise his voice
  • Speaks in concise, utilitarian statements
  • Reflexively identifies worst-case outcomes
  • Chronic pessimistic forecasting

Daeon's academic career was marked by complete scholastic dominance, achieved through obsessive study habits that permanently damaged his eyesight. He now requires glasses for vision correction, though he treats them as purely functional rather than aesthetic.

Core Value: Strategy as supremacy; order imposed on chaos through intellect

Business Operations

Despite his emotional distance, Daeon maintains select professional relationships. He facilitates distribution operations for Kayre's fashion enterprise through corporate infrastructure and logistical oversight.

Trivia: Daeon's companies incorporate "Day" in their names from the phonetic pronunciation of "Dae."

Combat Profile

The Starlit Czar represents strategic supremacy within Starlite 7th. His battlefield planning consistently produces decisive victories, and the rare defeats suffered by the team are overwhelmingly linked to his absence or premature removal from engagement.

Autarch Judicators

A paired set of reinforced katars designed for precision striking and close-quarters combat.

  • Optimized for rapid striking sequences
  • Built to channel kinetic force efficiently
  • Designed to pair with portal generation for extended reach

Daeon integrates his background in boxing into his fighting style, emphasizing centerline dominance, measured efficient movement, and devastating precision-based strikes.

Starlight Blessing: Portal Generation

Function: Allows the creation of spatial portals directly from Czar's fists, extending strike range beyond physical proximity.

  • Converts melee attacks into long-range impacts
  • Enables unpredictable attack vectors
  • Allows instantaneous repositioning of strike origin
  • Supports layered assault formations

The Blessing effectively transforms Czar into a spatial pressure fighter capable of delivering attacks from impossible angles.

Strategic Capabilities

  • Tactical scenario modeling
  • Multi-variable threat prediction
  • Formation optimization
  • Coordinated assault sequencing

Daeon's plans often account for contingencies several layers deep, enabling the team to adapt dynamically to chaotic battlefield conditions. He views combat as a problem-solving exercise rather than a contest of strength.
