Avant-Garde Composition

Era: Beeston Arc 2
Featured: Heart Seria, dissonant discord
Type: Final Act

There's an unexpected side of recurring nightmares.

Specifically, when you have the same nightmare over and over and over again, you might not immediately recognize when your nightmare is in fact happening in reality. So it is that Kali did not immediately process the news of Mahana being in active combat with dissonant discord. Granted, it didn't take long for her to spring into action: even in dreams, the urge to protect the girl who reminded her oh so much of her beloved sister was overwhelming to compelling.

Play, orchestra of love!
Heart Seria, The earnest prayer that heals the soul!

Sing, For a divine future!
We are,
HEART MELODY, LOVELY HEARTS!

She is the last of the team to transform and soon finds the other girls gathered in a circle. She takes her place and begins to hum the aria to activate teleportation magic. Sonata makes it a duet, including themes to help locate the missing Symphony. Rhythm and Verismo join the ensemble, adding depth and complexity to the sound whilst stabilizing the spell structure and locking down their teleportation location. Adagio comes in last with a piercing note to complete the sound and activate the spell. Heart Melody teleports away to join their new leader in her desperate struggle.

The battlefield is not a stage; it is a wound in reality.

When Heart Melody coalesces from the shimmering resonance of their teleportation, the air itself feels lacerated. The expected symphony of combat has been replaced by a terminal, deafening roar. Above the scorched earth, Finalis Grande is less a magical girl and more a localized apocalypse. Ambrasis has long since ceased to be a sword, appearing now as a jagged constellation of strike vectors that scream through the air, attacking from every conceivable angle at once.

At the center of this storm, dissonant discord moves with a terrifying, metronomic grace. For the first time, her composed smile is gone, replaced by the hyper-intelligent intensity of a predator finally meeting a force that can push back. She pivots on a hair's breadth, her slashing epee, Atonality, carving wide, shining arcs through the air to intercept the omnidirectional barrage of Finalis's will. Every time the blades clash, a ripple of gravitational distortion tears furrows into the ground, desynchronizing the very atoms of the surrounding space.

Finalis Grande is a terminal crescendo that refuses to resolve. She moves in violent, explosive propulsion bursts, launching herself through discord's dark magic with the sheer density of her radiant aura. She unleashes sustained barrages of magic: explosive waves and concussive detonations that bypass all traditional incantation. The two are locked in a cycle of catastrophic escalation: discord attempts to find the rhythm in the chaos to break it, while Finalis simply drowns the rhythm in a flood of unrestrained wrath.

But the cost of divinity is absolute.

As Finalis lunges for a strike that would shatter the horizon, her radiant light stutters. The atmospheric pressure drops. The immense magical expenditure required to maintain the Finalis state finally hits its limit, and her form flickers, the golden brilliance of Ambrasis dimming for a fraction of a second. She stumbles, her propulsion burst misfiring into a harmless hiss of steam.

In that heartbeat of instability, discord strikes. There is no theatricality now, only the cold, strategic patience of a killer. She steps into the collapse of Finalis's tempo, Atonality leveled for a precise, lethal severing of the girl's magical construct.

"Go now to sleep eternal," discord breathes, her voice a calm vacuum in the noise. "Fare thee well."

The shining blade flashes forward, a sliver of gravitational inevitability aimed directly at Finalis's heart.

CLANG.

The sound is not the scream of tearing magic, but the heavy, grounded thud of metal meeting an immovable object.

Heart Seria is there. She has not just moved; she has anchored herself into the very fabric of the battlefield. Cadence Bastion is raised, the madavu shield glowing with the steady, pulsing with Étheral light. The tip of discord's epee grinds against the shield's face, its dark resonance sparking harmlessly against Seria's layered barriers.

Seria's eyes are not the eyes of a healer or a gentle guardian. They are burning with a cold, jagged fury that mirrors the loss of Heart Aria. Her stance is low, her spatial awareness absolute as she expands her kinetic redirection fields to wrap around the flickering Finalis, stabilizing her leader's failing resonance. She smiles. It's feral.

"I have been waiting so long to kill you," Seria hisses, her voice trembling with the weight of her fury.

With a roar of effort, she executes a controlled shield strike, thrusting Cadence Bastion forward to displace discord and forces her back a step. Only one step, but leagues better than their last encounter.

discord's grin is back and more manic than ever.

"The kitten thinks she is a lion. Fine."

She levels her blade at Seria.

"I'll show you just how weak you truly are."

Seria knows it's a mistake. She knows she can't win. But if she gives up on trying to survive this fight, she might be able to take discord with her.

Seria throws up a barrier to keep the rest of the Lovely Hearts away.

The air within the barrier becomes a suffocating vacuum of gravitational pressure. Seria plants her feet in her pink boots, her Cadence Bastion glowing with a desperate, overdriven radiance of Éthera magic. She lunges, swinging the heavy madavu shield in a wide, rotational deflection designed to shatter discord's posture.

The antagonist moves with terrifying grace. discord doesn't retreat; she pivot just enough to allow the shield to whistle past her by a fraction of an inch. With a flick of her wrist, Atonality carves a dark arc through the air, hitting at the very core of Seria's magic. The blade's gravitational resonance fractures Seria's defensive field upon contact.

Seria pushes through a localized void distortion that tears at the hem of her skirt, manifesting a kinetic redirection field to catch the follow-up. She is fighting with a feral intensity, but discord is merely toying with her. The teal and black-clad enigma maintains a composed, manic smile below her mask while executing high-speed slashes that unfold as natural extensions of her swordplay.

Seria's breath comes in ragged hitches, her hair wild beneath her hood. She throws her shield forward in one last, desperate momentum check, pouring every ounce of her remaining Lúmenai into the impact. discord catches Cadence Bastion by one of its sharp spikes with her gloved hand.

The shockwave shatters the ground. Before Seria can react, discord's epee flashes in a blur, shattering the invisible magic Seria had been using to guide the shield. The Cadence Bastion clatters to the earth. discord's hand snaps forward, her fingers locking around Seria's throat.

The hapless guardian is hoisted into the air, her boots dangling. discord leans in, her mask's white eyes glowing as she gazes up at the choking girl.

"Your song is but a whisper in the dark," discord says, her voice a calm vacuum. "A broken note that falls before the spark."

Seria's barrier shudders, cracks, and finally dissolves into shimmering dust. Her vision begins to go dark, but snaps back as falls, no, is tossed aside to the ground. She can just make out the other Hearts rushing discord, Finalis having recovered enough to lead the charge.

The world shifts.

It's enough to give everyone pause.

discord lets out a joyful laugh.

"Ha ha! It worked. It actually worked! Well well."

The shift is dramatic. Gone is the bloodlust and intensity. Gone is the hostility. In its place… jubilation? discord's smile is no longer predatory. It's just happy.

"Welcome to Nexsicon, your new home. Beeston has been destroyed now, so you won't be going back. Accept it or don't. But I suggest you get stronger. I'm not the strongest one in these parts. Oh and lest I forget."

The now cheerful magical assassin walks over to Kali (when did she detransform?) and lifts her by the collar.

"You've been a huge pain and I'd really rather now have to deal with you. So, you gots ta go."

And discord throws her into a portal. Kali lands in a realm of darkness and she can hear countless creatures scuttling about. Though she can't see her she can hear discord's parting words.

"Welcome to the Void. I'd say try to survive but honestly? I kinda hope you don't."

And so ends the realm of Beeston.

dissonant discord stands triumphant.
